Set Up Your Apify Account

Follow each step in order. This only needs to be done once.

Step 1

Create your Apify account

›

Click the button below to open the Apify sign-up page. You can sign up with your Google account for the fastest experience, or use your email and create a password.

ℹ️Use your Carousell email Sign up with your Carousell Google account so everything stays organized and easy to find later.

2

Step 2

Confirm your email

›

Apify will send a confirmation email to your inbox. Open it and click the link inside to verify your account. Check your Spam folder if you don't see it within a few minutes.

⚠️Don't skip this step Without email confirmation, Apify will restrict your account from running any scraper tasks.
3

Step 3

Open your API & Integrations settings

›

Once logged in, go to your account settings and open the "API & Integrations" tab. This is where you'll find the two things you need to send to Christian: your User ID and your API token.

🔗 Open API & Integrations →
A Your Apify User ID    B Your Personal API token (hidden for security)

The page should look like the screenshot above. You'll see your Apify user ID at the top and your Default API token below it.

4

Step 4

Copy your User ID and API token

›

From the page you just opened, you need to copy two things:

1️⃣Your Apify User ID Found near the top of the page under "API tokens." It looks like a short string of letters and numbers (e.g., v1MjncrYJeKmtpTM6). Click the copy icon next to it.
2️⃣Your Default API Token Found just below, under "Personal API tokens." Click the eye icon to reveal it, then copy the full token string.
🔒Your API token is a password Never paste it into a public Slack channel, email, or chat. Send it to Christian via Slack direct message only.
5

Step 5

Send your details to Christian and request access

›

Copy the message template below, fill in your details, and send it to Christian via Slack DM. He'll set up your account in the system and send you your personal scraper form link.

Hi Christian, I've set up my Apify account and I'd like to request access to the Lead Scraper. Here are my details: • Name: [Your full name] • Team: [Your team, e.g. Sales SG / Sales HK / Sales PH] • Apify User ID: [Paste your User ID here] • Apify API Token: [Paste your API token here - Slack DM only] Please let me know once my access is set up. Thanks!
⚠️Do NOT upgrade your Apify plan Stay on the free plan. The $5/month credit that comes with the free account is all you need. Upgrading to "Starter" or any paid plan is unnecessary and will charge your card.
🎉

Setup complete!

Christian will confirm your access shortly. Once you have your form link, head to Guide 2 to learn how to run the scraper.

Column What it contains Can it be blank?

Step 4

Understanding your monthly budget

›

Each scraper run costs a small amount on your Apify account. The free plan includes a $5 USD monthly credit, which resets automatically every month. Here's what that looks like in practice.

How the $5 budget works

Each run scrapes up to 300 leads and costs roughly $1.20 USD.

$0$5.00 limit

That gives you roughly 4 runs per month, or up to 1,500 leads, before the budget is used up.

The budget resets automatically every month. If you hit the limit early, just wait for the reset and you can run again.

✓No action needed when the budget resets Apify handles this automatically. You'll see the usage go back to $0.00 on your reset date.
⚠️If you hit $5.00 before the reset Let Christian know and he can look at running it from a separate account. Don't upgrade your plan yourself.
